Lasagna Recipe

How to make Lasagna ?

Lasagna is a great Italian dish which is almost everyone's favourite. Below is the list of ingredients that you would need to make it:

  • Lasagna
  • Cheese of your choice
  • tomatoe sauce

Steps:

  1. Take few strips of Lasagna and add some tomatoe sauce on it
  2. Top it up with cheese
  3. put that topped Lasagna in the oven for 20 mins.
  4. Take it out after 20 mins and cut it in the pieces you like and its ready to eat.
