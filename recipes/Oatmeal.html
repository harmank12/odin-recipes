Oatmeal Recipe


How to make an Oatmeal ?

Oatmeal is the best breakfast that one can have and you can alter it as per your preference. Below is the list of required ingredients to make a delicious oatmeal.

  • Rolled Oats
  • Water or your choice of Milk
  • Honey or your choice of sweetner
  • Walnuts
  • Your choice of fruits

Steps:

  1. Add Oats and water/milk to the heated pan.
  2. keep stirring until you get the consistency that you like or atleast till 3 mins to soften the oats.
  3. Turn off the gas and pour the oatmeal in a bowl.
  4. Add walnuts and the fruits you like.
  5. Top it up with honey or your choice of sweetner.
  6. Your healthiest best Oatmeal breakfast is ready!
